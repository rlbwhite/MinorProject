What is ClearView?

ClearView is an automated planner that aims to work alongside online learning platforms, such as Moodle. It will aid students by showing at a glance, on any device, 24/7 - their progress throughout the course and help them plan their studies accordingly.

All course units, workshops, assessments, grading information, timelines and deadlines will be presented in a useful and editable timeline, on a single sign-on; one-page responsive website. This means users no longer have to spen unnecessary time orientating themselves with LMS structrue and information searching across multiple pages as the course requirements are now explicit, through ClearView - 24/7, over mobil, tablet or PC - leaving them more time to concentrate on content learning and accreditation success.

'ClearView' was born with one simple goal in mind, Improving the user experience of LMS users worldwide with a simple yet powerful data rich responsive website.
